WHO WE ARE

Pioneers in Nationwide Logistics Solutions

SkyNet Express Ltd, a wholly Ghanaian owned courier company was incorporated in February 2007 as a Company Limited by Liability and has been in operation since its establishment. The company is duly registered with all the appropriate government agencies including, Postal and Courier Services Regulatory Commission of Ghana, the Social Security and National Insurance Trust (SSNIT), Value Added Tax (VAT) and Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA)

Today, we operate in all 16 regions with over 12 offices nationwide, employing more than 300 logistics professionals dedicated to moving your business forward. Our integrated approach combines cutting-edge technology with deep industry expertise to deliver seamless logistics experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to become a premier courier company providing top-quality reliable services to corporate bodies. We seek to assist in expediting the collection, conveyance and distribution/delivery of courier items making it an easy and stress free experience. SkyNet seeks to be viewed as a highly visible company in the courier industry and will work to keep employees satisfied in order to maintain impeccable customer service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted logistics partner nationwide, recognized for our commitment to operational excellence, sustainable transportation, and delivering consistent value to our clients. We aim to lead the logistics industry by providing innovative supply chain solutions, ensuring timely and secure deliveries, and fostering long-term partnerships that drive nationwide commerce forward.

Our Promise

To deliver reliable, transparent, and customized logistics solutions that exceed expectations while building lasting partnerships.

OUR HISTORY

Milestones in Our Journey

From humble beginnings to global logistics leader

1995

Company Founded

SkyNet Express Ltd was established in Miami with just 5 employees, focusing on regional trucking services.

2002

First Nationwide Office

Expanded operations to Mexico City, marking our first step toward becoming a global logistics provider.

2008

Technology Revolution

Launched our proprietary logistics platform, revolutionizing how customers track and manage shipments.

2015

Nationwide Expansion

Established operations in Asia and Europe, completing our transformation into a truly global logistics network.

2023

Sustainability Leadership

Achieved carbon-neutral certification across all operations, leading the industry in green logistics solutions.
