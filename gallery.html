OUR HIRING PROCESS

How We Hire

We've designed our hiring process to be transparent, efficient, and fair. Here's what you can expect when you apply for a position at SkyNet Express.

1

Application Review

Our HR team reviews all applications carefully. We look for candidates whose skills and experience align with the position requirements.

Initial Screening

Selected candidates will have a phone or video call with HR to discuss the role, your experience, and answer any questions you may have.

2
3

Skills Assessment

Depending on the role, you may complete a skills test, case study, or technical assessment to evaluate your abilities.

Interview

You'll meet with the hiring manager and potentially other team members to discuss the role in detail and assess mutual fit.

4
5

Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates receive an offer and begin our comprehensive onboarding program to set you up for success in your new role.
