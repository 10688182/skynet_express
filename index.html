About Our Company

SkyNet Express Ltd, a wholly Ghanaian owned courier company was incorporated in February 2007 as a Company Limited by Liability and has been in operation since its establishment. The company is duly registered with all the appropriate government agencies including, Postal and Courier Services Regulatory Commission of Ghana, the Social Security and National Insurance Trust (SSNIT), Value Added Tax (VAT) and Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA)

Our mission is to become a premier courier company providing top-quality reliable services to corporate bodies. We seek to assist in expediting the collection, conveyance and distribution/delivery of courier items making it an easy and stress free experience. SkyNet seeks to be viewed as a highly visible company in the courier industry and will work to keep employees satisfied in order to maintain impeccable customer service.
