PHARMA-LOGISTICS & HEALTH DISTRIBUTION

At Skynet Express Ltd, we play a vital role in Ghana’s healthcare logistics ecosystem. We’ve partnered with institutions like CHI Medical, Nationwide Mutual Healthcare, C&J Pharmaceuticals, and Rimal Ghana Limited to ensure timely and secure delivery of critical medical equipment, laboratory supplies, and diagnostic films across the country. From temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals to general hospital supplies, we ensure safe, trackable, and compliant last-mile delivery.

  • Temperature-controlled medical and drug deliveries

  • Nationwide same-day and next-day distribution

  • Door-to-door delivery with real-time tracking

  • Licensed courier services for regulated health commodities

OIL & GAS LOGISTICS

Skynet Express Ltd supports Ghana’s energy sector through efficient and secure logistics solutions tailored for oil marketing and engineering companies. We transport fuel dispensing machines, critical components, and boxes of lubricant oils to and from filling stations and distribution centers nationwide.

Our trusted partnerships include Ghana Oil Company Limited (GOIL), Cardinal Engineering, Oando Ghana, Engen Ghana Limited, Star Oil, and Universal Engineering — ensuring seamless supply chain operations for the nation’s fuel infrastructure.

  • Safe transport of heavy and sensitive fuel dispensing machinery

  • Coordinated delivery across all fuel station zones

  • Real-time location and delivery tracking

  • Timely and dependable just-in-time delivery model

AUTOMOTIVE LOGISTICS

For over 15 years, SkyNet Express Ltd has been a strategic logistics partner to Ghana’s growing automobile industry. As Ghana’s auto market evolves—projected to surpass USD 10.6 billion by 2027—we have scaled our capabilities to serve both new and used vehicle sectors.

With automotive assembly plants from brands like Volkswagen, Nissan, and Toyota now operating locally, SkyNet Express plays a vital role in transporting vehicle parts and components across the country—ensuring timely delivery and robust supply chain support for manufacturers, dealers, and service centers.

  • End-to-end vendor coordination and part consolidation

  • Nationwide transportation and warehousing management

  • Demand forecasting and inventory optimization

  • Reverse logistics and returns management for auto parts

TELECOMMUNICATION LOGISTICS

The telecommunications industry is a critical driver of Ghana’s digital economy, supported by a liberalized market since 2007. With major investments flowing into ICT and communications, SkyNet Express plays a strategic logistics role in facilitating seamless operations within the sector.

From warehousing and last-mile distribution of telecom equipment to expedited delivery of SIM cards and devices, we support key telecom providers with reliable, tech-enabled logistics solutions. Backed by recent investments in system digitalization and customer experience, SkyNet Express is well-positioned to scale with the industry's anticipated growth.

Over the next five years, we plan to expand our nationwide branch network to meet the increasing demands of telecom partners—delivering speed, security, and efficiency at every stage.

  • Same-day and next-day delivery for critical telecom components

  • Dedicated couriers for high-priority and time-sensitive shipments

  • Advanced tracking and delivery confirmation systems

  • Guaranteed service levels with money-back assurance

AGRICULTURAL LOGISTICS

SkyNet Express supports Ghana's agricultural sector by offering dedicated logistics solutions to agrochemical companies, seed producers, and farming equipment suppliers. We deliver agrochemicals for Adam West Africa to locations including Kpando and Tamale, directly from their warehouses in Tema.

For AgriSeed Limited, we ensure the safe and timely distribution of agro products and seeds from Accra to all regional capitals. Additionally, our partnership with Dizengoff West Africa involves transporting agricultural machinery to farm sites across the country.

Our reliable delivery systems, specialized handling, and nationwide coverage help optimize operations for agribusinesses—ensuring farming communities get the tools and materials they need on time.

  • Temperature-controlled transport for sensitive products

  • Certified handling of hazardous agrochemicals

  • Transport of oversized and heavy farming equipment

  • Secure delivery of high-value or confidential shipments

E-COMMERCE

The e-commerce sector in Ghana has seen exponential growth, driven by digital transformation and accelerated by the COVID-19 pandemic. As more consumers and businesses turn to online platforms and social media for trade, the need for fast, reliable, and scalable delivery services is greater than ever.

SkyNet Express partners with online businesses—ranging from major platforms to independent sellers—to provide complete e-commerce logistics solutions. From order fulfillment and warehousing to delivery and cash handling, we help streamline operations so sellers can focus on growth.

  • Fast and reliable nationwide delivery across Ghana

  • Secure e-commerce product warehousing and inventory control

  • Cash-on-delivery (COD) services with real-time updates

  • Instant remittance for social commerce transactions

  • Seamless API integration for order syncing and tracking
