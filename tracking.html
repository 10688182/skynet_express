Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shipment tracking

How do I track my shipment?

Simply enter your tracking number in the field above and click "Track Now". You'll see detailed information about your shipment's current status and location.

Where can I find my tracking number?

Your tracking number was provided when you created your shipment. You can find it in your waybill, or in your SkyNet Express account under "Dashboard".

Why isn't my tracking number working?

It may be due to wrong tracking number entered hence, check and enter the correct tracking number. If you still can't track your shipment after this time, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

How often is tracking information updated?

Tracking information is updated in real-time as your shipment moves through our network. Major status changes (like pickup, transit scans, and delivery) are updated immediately.

What does "In Transit" mean?

"In Transit" means your shipment is on its way to the next facility in our network. This could mean it's moving between cities, being transferred between vehicles, or waiting for the next scheduled departure.

Need Help With Your Shipment?

Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions about your shipment or tracking information.
